Ultimate Workout and Sleep Schedule for Optimal Health

Warm-Up (5-10 minutes)

  • Jumping Jacks
  • Arm Circles
  • Push-Up Plus
  • Shoulder Rolls

Legs Workouts

  • Dumbbell Squats (3 sets of 12-15 reps)
  • Dumbbell Deadlifts (3 sets of 12 reps)
  • Dumbbell Lunges (3 sets of 12 reps per leg)
  • Dumbbell Step-Ups (3 sets of 12 reps per leg)

Arm Workouts

  • Dumbbell Bicep Curls (3 sets of 12-15 reps)
  • Dumbbell Tricep Extensions (3 sets of 12-15 reps)
  • Dumbbell Hammer Curls (3 sets of 12-15 reps)
  • Dumbbell Kickbacks (3 sets of 12-15 reps)

Back Workouts

  • Dumbbell Rows (3 sets of 12-15 reps)
  • Dumbbell Deadlifts (3 sets of 12-15 reps)
  • Dumbbell Pullovers (3 sets of 12-15 reps)
  • Dumbbell Shrugs (3 sets of 12-15 reps)

Cool Down (5 minutes)

  • Stretching (Hamstrings, Quads, Shoulders)
  • Deep Breathing
